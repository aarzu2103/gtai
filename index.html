Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI web development services

How accurate is the AI in creating websites?

Our AI has a 95% accuracy rate in creating websites that match client requirements. It analyzes industry best practices, your business needs, and design preferences to create professional websites that require minimal adjustments.

Can I edit the website after it's created?

Yes! All our plans include 1 year of free editing. You can request changes to content, images, colors, and layout. We also provide a simple admin panel for minor updates you can do yourself.

What's included in the hosting package?

Our hosting includes unlimited bandwidth, SSL certificate, daily backups, 99.9% uptime guarantee, and technical support. The .in domain registration is also included for the first year.

How secure are the websites?

All websites are built with enterprise-level security features including SSL certificates, secure hosting, regular security updates, and protection against common web threats like SQL injection and XSS attacks.

Do you provide SEO services?

Yes, all websites come with built-in SEO optimization including meta tags, structured data, fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, and search engine friendly URLs. We also offer advanced SEO packages.

How long does it take to create a website?

AI-generated websites are ready within 24-48 hours. Custom websites take 3-5 business days depending on complexity. E-commerce sites may take 5-7 days for complete setup including payment integration.
